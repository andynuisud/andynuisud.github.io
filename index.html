About me

Hi, my name is Andrew Nuisud, but most people call me Andy. My journey into the world of technology began early on, fueled by a curiosity to understand and manipulate the underlying mechanisms of websites and online platforms. This fascination led me to explore various programming languages, including C#, C++, HTML, CSS, Java, Go, JavaScript, and Python. My passion for programming is not just a career path but a hobby that brings me joy and satisfaction.

My first foray into leveraging programming skills in a practical and impactful manner was through identifying and exploiting security vulnerabilities in websites. A notable example of this was on Roblox, where I created a trade bot for a game. This endeavor was not only a learning experience but also a financially rewarding one, as it earned me three thousand dollars during my sophomore year of high school. This early success was a testament to the potential of coding to transform ideas into tangible outcomes.

Participating in hackathons has been another avenue through which I have honed my skills and challenged myself. In 2023, I achieved a remarkable milestone by securing 3rd place at HPE's Codewars, a testament to my technical skills and ability to innovate under pressure.

As I continue to program for fun, my ultimate goal is to specialize as a machine learning software engineer. This ambition stems from a belief in the transformative power of technology and a desire to contribute to advancements in AI and machine learning. My journey in programming, from exploiting website vulnerabilities to competing in hackathons, has been a rich source of learning and growth. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the field of machine learning and am eager to leverage my diverse programming skills to make significant contributions to this dynamic and evolving domain.
